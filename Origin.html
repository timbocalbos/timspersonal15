Facts About The Land Of The Rising Sun:
The Kanji

             Bravery, Courage, Gallantry. That is the meaning of the symbol in my kanji logo because in a nutshell, it depicts what kind of person I am. First of all, I chose this kanji symbol because of its classic look on the eyes and simplicity. It really strike me due to its mysterious content and of course it is a Japanese character which I like the most. I am thinking so profoundly on what to choose as a symbol because I really don’t know how to create one because im not that pro enough. This kanji character shows the primordial years of Japanese people and their creative thinking on words. As a anime fan, I was accustomed to their traditions on writing and learn how to speak some of their mother tongue. I think my addiction for Japanese culture started when I was a grade 5 student, beacause I love watching anime especially Katekyo Hitman Reborn, that was my first love. Without further ado, I will tell you why this kanji represents my personality.

              For so many years, I’ve come to experience hardships and inequalities in life. That’s what I am sure that life is not fair in all perspectives. I was so a bit confused and depressed back then because I cannot cope with the people’s expectation on you. That’s why I created my own world by being independent and strategic. I just set a disciplinary act in every move I make because it will prevent me on doing wrong, just like a famous quotations said “Silence is the most powerful scream”.

             Lastly, hardships and obstacles become my training ground in life that’s why I became a courageous person and always faces challenges because it would make me grow someday. To end the message of my logo, I strongly believe that “It is not how hard you take the hit of the challenge in life but it is how you get up on your knee and retaliate with full force in order to regain your honor”. That’s why I believe that God will make things possible because He is always with me.
