The Funny One

                Her name is Lola Natividad or I call her Mama Ti, for the reason that she is my primary caregiver when I was still a young ball of happiness, a child. From the beginning when I was born, she is always there to guide and spoil me when my father doesn’t want me to have powdered milk without water. I can say that I am a lola’s boy and they say that a lola’s boy is the best lovers when they have relationship. I guess so because I am a lovable person and a good humor to begin with. My lola always makes fun of me everytime and we are just like “tropa” in the house but of course having a fine line between a grandmother and a grandchild that requires high respect to each other.
