A Must Wanderlust



       I also like to travel around the world! Specially in European countries, I’ve written many countries to visit in my bucket list but there’s only 1 country I’ve visited recently. Though this webpage is Japanese themed, I really have to put my escapade in order to bring joy for the readers. The Land of the KPOP and KDRAMA’S, South Korea! It was a prodigious feeling for me when I was chosen to represent in the Imus-Yeoncheon Student Youth Exchange. During my stay in there, I’ve taken account some of their culture and their daring act when in the public bath. Yes its butt naked when you have to splash on the hot spring unless you’ll be kicked out because of being frivolous. I began to be acquainted with my Korean friend Lee Dae Young who looks like a Korean idol, because we’re both handsome, LOL. Likewise, we’ve tasted various Korean foods like bingsu, bibimbap, bulgogi, jajangmyeon and of course, their homemade-10-year-fermented Kimchi! Yep, it stinks but tastes good. We’ve also visited the renowned Namsan Tower and Myeongdong, the local Divisoria of South Korea. Someday I will come back and try some new things there with my family and friends.
