Feast for the Beast

                Food, of course is the mood stabilizer of all people due to its satisfying effect in our body and in psychology, food makes us sane. My family loves to eat in buffets and I tell you, we are the best in eating in buffets because of the different strategies we make. My father taught me not to drink water in order not to get full in this buffet and you’ll eat a lot more because of that. There’s a time where we ate in the Chicken All You Can at Max’s. My brother and I just dominated their game by eating 17 quarters of chicken in one sitting.
